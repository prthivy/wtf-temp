What The Fudge!?

Handcrafted, semi-solid fudge that makes you say... well, you know.

The Original Sin

Our journey began with a timeless classic. Rich, dark chocolate, melted to perfection and blended with fresh cream and butter. It's the fudgiest fudge you'll ever fudge, setting the bar for everything that followed. A true original.

Salted Caramel Seduction

Experience the perfect dance of sweet and savory. We swirl our creamy caramel fudge with flakes of gourmet sea salt. Each bite is a delightful surprise, a flavor explosion that will keep you coming back for just one more piece.

Peanut Butter Panic

For the nut lovers, we present a creation that's pure bliss. We take creamy, dreamy peanut butter and whip it into our signature fudge base. It's nutty, it's smooth, and it's dangerously delicious. Don't panic, there's plenty to go around.

White Chocolate Raspberry

Elegance in a bite. We blend decadent white chocolate with a tart raspberry swirl, creating a flavor profile that is both sophisticated and irresistible. It's a fruity, creamy delight that looks as good as it tastes.

Espresso Shot Shock

Get your buzz on with our coffee-infused creation. We fold finely ground espresso beans into our dark chocolate fudge for a rich, aromatic experience that will awaken your senses. It's the perfect pick-me-up for any time of day.

Mint Chocolate Mayhem

A refreshing classic with a decadent twist. Cool, crisp mint is perfectly balanced with our rich dark chocolate fudge. It's an invigorating and satisfying treat that cleanses the palate while indulging your sweet tooth.

Our Story

It all started in a small kitchen with a big mess.

What The Fudge!? was born from a simple, yet profound, question: "Why is it so hard to find truly great fudge?" We were tired of the grainy, overly-sweet stuff. We craved fudge that was smooth, rich, and made with ingredients you could actually pronounce.

So, we took matters into our own hands. After countless hours of testing, tasting, and tweaking (and a few moments that made us say... well, you know), we perfected our recipes. We use real butter, fresh cream, premium chocolate, and a whole lot of love. No shortcuts, no compromises.

We're a small team with a big passion for confectionery perfection. Every batch is handcrafted and taste-tested to ensure it meets our ridiculously high standards. We believe fudge shouldn't just be a treat; it should be an experience. Thanks for being part of our sweet journey.
